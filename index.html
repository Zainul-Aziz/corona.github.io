About COVID-19

What is Covid-19 (Coronavirus)

Covid-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus.This new virus and disease were known before the outbreak began in Wuhan,China, in December 2019

Coronavirus are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in animals or humans. In humans,several coronavirus are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Sysndrome (SARS).The most recently discovered coronavirus causes Coronavirus disease Covid-19.

Steps For Prevention Of Coronavirus

Wash your hands regularly with soap and water for atleast 20 seconds to remove germs.

Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or elbow while sneezing.Make use of disposable mask.

Avoid close contact with people who are sick. Maintain social distancing in public places.
